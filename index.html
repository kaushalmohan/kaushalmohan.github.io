Welcome to My Page

Hi! I'm Kaushal Mohan, and I'm a Computer Science Engineer from PES Institute of Technology, Bangalore. Over the last couple of years or so, I've been doing some really cool stuff at Citrix R&D - I've worked on OpenSSL, OpenStack, Load Balancers, and have built testing frameworks for different products from scratch.

I enjoy anything that involves me speaking - presentations, debates, talks. I love writing, and have been doing it for as long I remember - in my school days I was part of the editorial board of the school magazine, and then I followed it up by writing for the college newsletter. During my college days, I also wrote for Sportskeeda and Cricket Country, where I interviewed prominent sports personalities, covered events, wrote articles about events on and off the field, and penned down pieces about present and past cricketers.

I've lived in Bangalore all my life, and I love the place to bits. However, I love exploring new places and have been fortunate to have travelled to a few countries until now, and I look forward to adding to that list.
